Lasagne

Lasagne is a dish indigenous to Italy, but enjoyed all over the globe. Italian immigrants to America have further developed the dish with the addition of a beschmel sauce instead of ricotta

An ingredients list for this meal is shown below

  • Mince meat
  • Tin of Tomatoes
  • Lasgne Pasta sheets

Steps

  1. Put 125g of butter into a pot and melt it
  2. Once melted, place 63g of flour into the pot and stir. It is important to keep stirring or else it will burn
  3. Cook for 3 minutes and then add in 4 cups of milk slowly, careful not to create clumps
  4. Cook for 15 minutes, and then add salt, pepper, freshly ground nutmeg and parmessan cheese
  5. Place into a bowl with clingfilm on top. Press the clingfilm down to the sauce, and place in the fridge for 30 minutes to cool
